As a brand new front end engineer, I am excited to embark on a journey of growth and learning in the field of web development. With a degree in graphic design, I am already skilled in user experience, and am eager to bring my skills and enthusiasm to a future team of developers. I hope to someday join a team of like-minded professionals who share my passion for technology, design, and making a positive impact on the world.
